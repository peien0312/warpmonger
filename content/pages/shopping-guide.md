## Shopping Guide

New to Warpmonger? Here's everything you need to know about shopping with us.

### How to Order

1. **Browse** our product catalog and find items you love.
2. **Add to List** - Click "Add to List" on any product page.
3. **Review your List** - Visit your Shopping List to review selected items.
4. **Send your List** - Send your shopping list via LINE for a quote and order confirmation.

### Payment Methods

- Bank Transfer
- LINE Pay
- Other methods available upon request

Please contact us via LINE for payment details after submitting your order.

### Shipping

- We ship to Taiwan and select international destinations.
- **Domestic (Taiwan):** Standard shipping takes **1-3 business days**.
- **International:** Shipping times vary by destination. Please inquire for details.
- Shipping fees are calculated based on order weight and destination.

### Pre-Order Items

- Some products are available for pre-order before their official release.
- Pre-order items will ship once they arrive in stock.
- Expected availability dates are listed on each product page.
- Pre-order payments are required at the time of ordering.

### Product Condition

- All products are **brand new and sealed** unless otherwise stated.
- We source from authorized distributors and manufacturers.

### Contact & Support

- **LINE:** Add us for the fastest response.
- **Email:** Available for inquiries and support.
- Response time: Within **1 business day**.

We're here to help you find the perfect collectible!
