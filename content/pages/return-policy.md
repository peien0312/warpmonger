## Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please read our return and exchange policy below.

### Eligibility

- Items must be returned within **14 days** of delivery.
- Products must be in their **original, unopened packaging** and in unused condition.
- Items that have been opened, assembled, or damaged by the buyer are **not eligible** for return.

### How to Request a Return

1. Contact us via **LINE** or **Email** with your order details.
2. Include your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason.
3. We will respond within **1-2 business days** with return instructions.

### Refund Process

- Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within **5-7 business days**.
- Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.
- Shipping costs are **non-refundable** unless the return is due to our error.

### Exchanges

- If you'd like to exchange an item, please contact us and we'll help arrange a swap for an available product of equal value.
- Price differences will be charged or refunded accordingly.

### Damaged or Defective Items

- If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us **within 3 days** of receiving it with photos of the damage.
- We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost.

### Contact Us

For any return or exchange inquiries, please reach out via LINE or email. We're happy to help!
