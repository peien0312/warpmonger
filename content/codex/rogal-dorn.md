Rogal Dorn was the Primarch of the Imperial Fists Legion and one of the Emperor's most loyal sons. A master of defensive warfare, Dorn was chosen to fortify Terra itself against the traitor onslaught during the Horus Heresy. He stood beside the Emperor in the final battle aboard Horus's flagship and witnessed the terrible confrontation that left the Emperor interred upon the Golden Throne.
